Sudoku Solver

Instructions

Enter the numbers of your unsolved Sudoku puzzle in the grid above.

Leave cells blank for empty positions or use 0.

Click "Solve" to find the solution.

Click "Clear" to reset the grid.

Click "Load Example" to load a sample puzzle.

Click "Extract from Clipboard" to extract a Sudoku puzzle from a screenshot in your clipboard. This requires a modern browser with Clipboard API support, and you'll need to have an image of a Sudoku puzzle copied to your clipboard. You may be prompted to allow clipboard access.
