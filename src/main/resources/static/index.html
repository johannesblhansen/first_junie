Instructions

Enter the numbers of your unsolved Sudoku puzzle in the grid above.

Leave cells blank for empty positions or use 0.

Click "Solve" to find the solution.

Click "Clear" to reset the grid.

Click "Load Example" to load a sample puzzle.

Click "Extract from Clipboard" to extract a Sudoku puzzle from a screenshot in your clipboard. This will first try to use your browser's clipboard access (you may be prompted for permission), and if that fails, it will use the server to access your clipboard.
